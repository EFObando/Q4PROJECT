About



Ryu "Keria" Min-seok (Hangul: 류민석; born in October 14, 2002) is a League of Legends esports player, currently support for T1.



His consistent efficiency in 2021 remarked Keria as the best support in the world. His 2020 previous team member Deft "lauded him as one of the best support players in South Korea before he had even debuted in pro play. Deft was right -- Keria is one of the best supports in South Korea.
